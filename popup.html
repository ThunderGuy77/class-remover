    How To Use

    Enter the name of the class you'd like to remove and press "Remove Class". Refresh, and the class will be gone!

    For best results, try to match the name exactly. Unless you're purposely trying to get rid of your other classes too. You can use either the original name of the class or your custom name for the class to remove them from your dashboard.
